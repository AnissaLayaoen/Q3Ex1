My favourite "Frozen" Characters

Elsa

Elsa is the confident and resolved Snow Queen.

Anna

Anna is warm and optimistic. She will do anything for her sister, Elsa.

Olaf

Olaf is a very friendly snowman in Arendelle. He also really loves the warm weather.

Kristoff

Kristoff is Arendelle's official Ice Master. He is loyal and caring.
